# Re-learning React and Exploring Three.js and R3F

Through this repository, I am documenting my re-learning process of React, as I have previously worked with it but work and other commitments stirred me away from it for a while. I am excited to dive back into React and explore its features and capabilities once again. Also, the repository contains my structured approach to learning new libraries like Three and R3F.

By no means is this repository meant to be a comprehensive guide to neither React nor the other libraries I am learning, but rather a testament to my commitment to learning and my adaptability as a professional.

### Structure 

The repository contains three main folders:
* `Learning React`: This folder contains my notes, exercises, and projects related to React. It includes various subfolders for different topics such as components, hooks, state management, and more.
* `Learning Three.js`: This folder is dedicated to my exploration of Three.js. It includes examples, experiments, and projects that I am working on to understand the capabilities of Three.js.
* `Learning R3F`: This folder is dedicated to my exploration of R3F (React Three Fiber). It includes examples, experiments, and projects that I am working on to understand the capabilities of R3F.
